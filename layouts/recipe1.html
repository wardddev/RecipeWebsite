Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients
  • 1 cup of Unsalted Peanut Butter
  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1 cup of Chocolate
  • 6.5 tbsps of Almond Milk
  • 1 tsp of Baking Soda
  • 1 Pinch of Salt

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix Peanute Butter and Sugar
  3. Add in Almond Milk
  4. Add in Flour, Baking Soda, and Salt
  5. Add Chocolate Chips
  6. Form dough balls and place on tray
  7. Bake for 9 min or until golden brown
